# Copy Rewrites — Before / After

## Error Messages

**Before (AI slop):**
> "Oops! Something went wrong. Please try again later. If the issue persists, contact support."

**After:**
> "Couldn't save your changes — the server timed out. Your work is still here. Try saving again, or [download a backup]."

---

**Before:**
> "Invalid input. Please check your entries and try again."

**After:**
> "Your email needs an @ sign — like name@example.com"

---

## Empty States

**Before:**
> "No items yet! Click the + button to create your first item and get started on your journey. 🚀"

**After:**
> "No campaigns yet. [Create your first campaign] — takes about 2 minutes."

---

## Onboarding

**Before:**
> "Welcome to Aria! We're so excited to have you here. Let's take a quick tour to get you up and running! This will only take a few minutes. ✨"

**After:**
> "What are you building first?" [three specific options, each leads to a different path]

---

## Feature Announcements

**Before:**
> "We're thrilled to announce a powerful new feature that will transform the way you collaborate! Stay tuned for more exciting updates coming soon!"

**After:**
> "You can now share a workspace with up to 50 guests without giving them full account access. Useful if you work with clients or contractors."

---

## CTAs

| Slop | Better | Why |
|---|---|---|
| "Get started" | "Create your account" | Specific |
| "Learn more" | "See how pricing works" | Answers the actual question |
| "Sign up free" | "Try it free — no card needed" | Removes the actual objection |
| "Submit" | "Send my message" | Human voice |
| "Upgrade" | "Unlock team features" | Names the benefit |
| "Cancel" | "Keep my subscription" | Reframes the loss |

**Before:**

## Confirmation Dialogs

**Before:**
> "Are you sure you want to delete this item? This action cannot be undone. Click OK to confirm."

**After:**
> **Delete "Q4 Campaign Plan"?**  
> This removes the file and all its history. [Delete permanently] [Cancel]

---

## Success Messages

**Before:**
> "Success! Your changes have been saved successfully."

**After:**
> "Saved" (inline, near the save button, fades in 1s)

Or with useful context:
> "Published — your changes are live at yoursite.com"

---

## Tooltips

**Before:**
> "Click here to access settings for this item."

**After:**
> "Edit privacy, labels, and sharing" (on hover for a ⚙ icon)

---

## Notifications

**Before:**
> "You have a new notification! Someone has interacted with your content."

**After:**
> "**Marta** commented on 'Rebrand brief': 'Can we revisit the color palette?'"

---

## AI Response Openers to Delete

Cut every response that starts with:
- "Great question!"
- "Certainly!"
- "Absolutely!"
- "Of course!"
- "Sure thing!"
- "I'd be happy to help with that."
- "That's a really interesting point."
- "I understand your concern."

Just start with the answer.

---

## The Word Purge List

These words should trigger a rewrite:

| Word | Why | Replace with |
|---|---|---|
| Seamless | Overused to meaninglessness | Specific friction removed |
| Intuitive | Patronizing and vague | Show it; don't claim it |
| Powerful | Every tool claims this | What specifically can it do? |
| Robust | Enterprise buzzword | Specific reliability claim |
| Leverage | Corporate verb | Use |
| Utilize | Unnecessary formality | Use |
| Streamline | Vague efficiency claim | Specific time/step saved |
| Innovative | Everyone says this | What is actually new? |
| Ecosystem | Startup inflation | Platform, suite, or just name the pieces |
| Journey | Your product is not a journey | Onboarding, process, path |
| Empower | Hollow activation verb | Specific capability granted |
| Holistic | Consulting speak | Comprehensive, or just describe it |
| Synergy | 2005 called | [delete] |
